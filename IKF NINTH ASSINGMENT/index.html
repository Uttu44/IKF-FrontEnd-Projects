About Us

We develop the most suitable and efficient solution for you WPIL Limited, incorporated in 1952, is a multinational flow solutions company, headquartered out of India, with diversified operations covering the entire gamut of the pumping industry maintaining a long-standing tradition of producing specialized, engineered industrial pumps for the core sector. The Company has to its credit 70 years of rich experience & legacy delivering flow solutions through - Designing, Developing, Manufacturing, Erecting, Commissioning and Servicing of Pumps & Pumping Systems.
